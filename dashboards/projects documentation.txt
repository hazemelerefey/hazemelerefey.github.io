Project 1: Healthcare Operations & Waitlist Dashboard
This project focused on operational efficiency and patient flow management, moving from raw data to actionable medical insights.
The Roadmap (Steps Taken)
1. Data Requirement Analysis: Identifying key healthcare metrics like patient wait times, department capacity, and age-based demographics.
2. ETL Pipeline (Power Query): Cleaning messy clinical data, handling null values in patient records, and standardizing "Case Types."
3. Dimensional Modeling: Building a Star Schema to link patient "Fact" tables with "Dimension" tables (Date, Department, and Patient Demographics).
4. Analytical Layer (DAX): Developing complex measures for Time-Band vs. Age Profiling to see which age groups waited the longest.
5. UI/UX Design: Creating a split-view dashboard that separates high-level KPIs from granular case-type details.
The Toolkit
* Power BI: For the core visualization and dashboard structure.
* DAX: To calculate dynamic waitlist trends and patient aging logic.
* Power Query (M): For the automated ETL processes.
* Star Schema: The underlying data architecture
* Power point : design the dashboard background








Project 2: Global E-Commerce Sales Tracker
This dashboard was designed for executive-level oversight, focusing on international market performance and financial health.

The Roadmap (Steps Taken)
Financial Mapping: Defining the core business metrics—Revenue, Profit Margins, and Total Order Volume.

Geographic Normalization: Ensuring all regional data (countries/cities) mapped correctly for map-based visualizations.

Data Integration: Consolidating sales data from multiple regions into a single, unified data model.

Interactive Logic: Implementing Geographic Drill-downs, allowing users to click a continent and see performance down to the specific city or product category.

Performance Optimization: Refining the model to ensure that large volumes of global sales data filtered instantly without lag.

The Toolkit
Power BI : For data hosting and visualization.

Power Query: To handle currency formatting and regional data cleaning.

Geographic Mapping Tools: Integrated within Power BI for the spatial drill-downs.

Summary of Your Skills Demonstrated
Data Architecture: Moving beyond simple spreadsheets to structured Star Schemas.

Business Intelligence: Converting raw numbers into "Profit Margins" and "Operational Efficiency."
